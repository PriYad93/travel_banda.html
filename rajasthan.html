Welcome to Rajasthan

Explore the rich culture, delicious food, vibrant dances, and beautiful tourist spots of Rajasthan.

Food of Rajasthan

Rajasthan is known for its royal and flavorful cuisine. Some of the popular dishes include:

  • Dal Baati Churma
  • Gatte ki Sabzi
  • Laal Maas
  • Pyaaz Kachori

Dance Forms of Rajasthan

Rajasthan is famous for its folk dance forms, which are lively and colorful.

Some of the famous dance forms include:

  • Ghoomar
  • Kalbeliya
  • Bhavai

Tourist Spots in Rajasthan

Rajasthan is home to many iconic tourist attractions, including:

  • Jaipur - The Pink City with its majestic palaces
  • Udaipur - Known as the City of Lakes
  • Jaisalmer - Famous for its golden desert and forts
  • Mount Abu - A serene hill station
